Drop your real test photos here, one subfolder per person.

  realfaces/
    alice/
      1.jpg        ← two different angles of the same person = best (matches production)
      2.jpg
    bob/
      front.jpg    ← a single photo is fine too; it just uses the 1-photo path
    you/
      a.jpg
      b.jpg

Then run (from the project root):

  node scripts/calibrate/gen.js --faces-dir scripts/calibrate/realfaces --samples 2 --yes

Notes:
- 2 photos per person → the harness fuses both (Flux kontext-multi), exactly like your
  live product. 1 photo → single-photo path (slightly lower likeness).
- The gate scores each render against the FIRST photo in the folder.
- jpg/png only. At least 768x768 (1024+ is better), same as the real uploader.
- You can mix real people here with a synthetic batch — just run gen.js twice into
  different --out folders, or label them in separate passes.
