    Questions

    Can I remove the bird?

    No. There is no button to remove the bird. There never was a button. The bird stays.

    Will it actually look like me?

    Yes — that's why we ask for two angles. The headshots are AI-generated using multiple inputs to improve quality. A real likeness is the whole point.

    What do I get for $1?

    Three professional headshots across three outfit-and-backdrop looks, each starring the one bird we've paired to you — plus a shareable Bird ID card.

    How long does it take?

    About a minute. No model training, no waiting hours like the other guys.

    What photos work best?

    Two clear, front-facing, well-lit shots from slightly different angles, at least 768px on the short side. JPG, PNG, or HEIC.

    Do you keep my photos?

    No. Your uploads are used to generate your headshots and then discarded — we don't store them. See our Privacy Policy.

    Can I choose my bird?

    No. The bird chooses you. There are 148 species; you get exactly one.

    Refunds?

    If we fail to deliver your headshots, you're refunded. Otherwise all sales — and all birds — are final.

    About

    We noticed AI headshot companies were charging 100x markup on a simple $1 API call and pocketing the rest. We decided to create an alternative that can rival their quality for much, much less. So we built it in 2 days, and it only costs one dollar.

    Why the bird? Frankly we're just as confused as you. Frontier AI models still struggle with specialised cognitive tasks. You see, to them, the notion of "thinking" is merely predicting patterns in data — in our case pixels. Our bot generates incredible headshots quickly, and at a cost, but always with a bird. We just decided to go with it. Long live bird bot.
