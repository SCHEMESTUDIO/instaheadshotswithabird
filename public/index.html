    Questions

    Wait — the bird is GONE?

    Contained, not gone. As of today your headshots come out clean — no bird in any of them, because that's what you kept asking for. The bird now lives on your shareable Bird ID card, added to the one headshot you pick, plus a separate “you + your bird” photo to download. The bird remains load-bearing. It's just been given an office.

    Can I get the bird IN a headshot?

    Yes — exactly one. After your headshots generate, pick your favourite and we add your bird to it: that photo appears on your Bird ID card and as its own download. The other headshots stay clean for LinkedIn, CVs, and other bird-hostile environments.

    Will it actually look like me?

    Yes — the headshots are AI-generated from your photo, and we keep your exact expression rather than inventing one. A real likeness is the whole point.

    What do I get for $1?

    Five clean professional headshots across five outfit-and-backdrop looks, a shareable Bird ID card starring the shot you pick (bird included), and a “you + your bird” photo.

    How long does it take?

    About a minute. No model training, no waiting hours like the other guys.

    What photos work best?

    One clear, front-facing, well-lit shot, at least 768px on the short side. JPG, PNG, or HEIC. Tip: your headshots will carry the same expression as your photo — so pick one where you look how you'd like your headshot to look.

    Do you keep my photos?

    No. Your uploads are used to generate your headshots and then discarded — we don't store them. See our Privacy Policy.

    Can I choose my bird?

    On the $10 Whole Aviary pack, yes — all 148 species, your call, drunk with power. On the $1 and $3 packs the bird chooses you, as nature intended.

    Why is my bird ENORMOUS?

    Because it's enormous in real life. Our 148 species include ostriches, condors, an emperor penguin, and a wandering albatross with a 3.5-metre wingspan. Small birds appear at polite, shoulder-adjacent scale. Birds that physics forbids from perching appear appropriately, alarmingly large. We could have shrunk them. We chose truth.

    Why only one photo? Other services want 10+.

    Because we tested it. One great photo beat two in our trials, and it beats making you dig up twelve. Services demanding 10+ selfies are training a custom model, which is why they take hours and cost $30+. We do something smarter, faster, and one dollar-er.

    Why is it so cheap?

    Because it's cheap to make and we're not pretending otherwise. Your five headshots cost us about 40 cents to generate — honestly, the payment processor takes a bigger cut than the AI does. We charge $1 because that covers it. That's the whole business model.

    Refunds?

    If we fail to deliver your headshots, you're refunded. Otherwise all sales — and all birds — are final.

    I miss the bird being in every shot.

    We understand. Progress is painful. Your Bird ID card and “you + your bird” photo are shareable precisely so the bird's legacy survives. If demand is loud enough we may offer a birds-in-everything heritage mode. Email us.

    About

    AI headshot companies are charging huge markup on simple $1 API calls. We decided to create an alternative that can rival their quality for much, much less. So we built it in 2 days, and it only costs one dollar.

    Why the bird? Frankly we're just as confused as you. For a while the bird appeared in every single headshot — non-negotiable, no button to remove it.
